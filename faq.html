Q.What is Ecommerce Website

Ans:A website that allows people to buy and sell physical goods, services, and digital
products over the internet rather than at a brick-and-mortar location.
Through an e-commerce website, a business can process orders, accept payments,
manage shipping and logistics, and provide customer service.
